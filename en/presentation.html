Who is Barpix Repair?

The story behind the workshop.

Hi, I'm Nicolas

A Nintendo fan since childhood, I started repairing consoles self-taught, driven by curiosity and the urge to understand what's under the hood.

Over time, this passion grew into real technical expertise, especially around capture card kits — a hardware mod that lets gamers and streamers record their sessions directly from their console.

Barpix Repair is a workshop where each console gets individual attention. No assembly line: every project is unique, handled with precision and carried out with transparency.

// My values

How I work

🔍

Honest diagnosis

Before any work, I fully assess the state of your console and give you a clear quote. No nasty surprises.

⚡

Responsiveness

I reply to requests quickly, usually within 24h. Turnaround depends on the project's complexity and is always announced in advance.

📦

Secure shipping

Delivery throughout France and Europe. UPS Business with insurance at the real value of your parcel for fast, secure shipping. Mondial Relay without insurance for low-cost delivery and easy pickup in a locker. Your console is always carefully packed to withstand any shock during transit.

💬

Real-time updates

During the repair or mod, I share photos and updates via Discord or e-mail. You always know where your console stands.

🎯

Capture card specialist

Installing capture card kits is my main activity. I master the 3DS, DS and several Nintendo consoles, and even the GBA SP via an HDMI kit.
