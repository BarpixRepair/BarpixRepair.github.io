Who is Barpix Repair?

The story behind the workshop.

🛠️

Hi, I'm Nicolas

A Nintendo fan since childhood, I started repairing consoles self-taught, driven by curiosity and the urge to understand what's under the hood.

Over time, this passion grew into real technical expertise, especially around capture card kits — a hardware mod that lets gamers and streamers record their sessions directly from their console.

Barpix Repair is a workshop where each console gets individual attention. No assembly line: every project is unique, handled with precision and carried out with transparency.
