Capture Card Installation

Barpix Repair's flagship mod. Integrate a capture card directly into your console to stream or record your sessions.

New 3DS XL 🎮

Internal capture card kit installation. Enjoy the latest kit on the market (Loopy 2024) to capture audio and video via USB to your PC. Clean, stable signal with no in-game latency.

DS 🎮

Installation adapted to the original DS. Signal also transmitted over USB. Identical result to the 3DS and lets you enjoy GBA and DS games.

⚙️ How does it work?

The capture card is an electronic component built inside the console, with a USB type-C port to connect your console to your PC. It intercepts the video signal before it reaches the screen and sends it via USB to your PC. You can then stream on Twitch, YouTube, record your sessions or create content without an external box. The console charges through the USB cable, no charger needed. The software to capture the stream is 3DS Capture or cc3dsfs, preferred when using several consoles.
