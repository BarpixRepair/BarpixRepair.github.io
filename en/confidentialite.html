Privacy policy

Barpix Repair takes the protection of your personal data seriously. This policy explains what data is collected, why, and what your rights are, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and French data-protection law.

Data controller

Nicolas Garnier, as publisher of Barpix Repair. Contact: barpixrepair@gmail.com.

Data collected and purposes

  • Quote request / contact form: the information you provide (name or alias, e-mail address, details about your console or project). Purpose: answer your request, prepare a quote and follow up on the service.
  • Communication via Discord: if you contact us through our Discord server, the exchanges are also processed by Discord under its own terms.
  • Audience measurement: statistical tools may be used to understand site traffic. They are only activated after your consent (see "Cookies").

Legal basis

Processing of contact/quote requests is based on pre-contractual measures taken at your request and/or on our legitimate interest in responding. Audience measurement is based on your consent.

Recipients

Your data is intended solely for Barpix Repair. It is never sold, rented or transferred to third parties for commercial purposes. Only technical providers strictly necessary for the site (host, consent management and, where applicable, the audience-measurement tool) may access it.

Transfers outside the EU

As the site is hosted by GitHub (USA) and some technical tools may be located outside the EU, transfers of data outside the European Union may occur. They are governed by appropriate safeguards (notably the European Commission's standard contractual clauses).

Retention period

  • Contact/quote requests without a contractual relationship: 3 years maximum from the last contact.
  • Documents related to a completed service: in line with applicable legal (notably accounting) obligations.
  • Audience measurement: tracker lifespan limited to 13 months, data kept for 25 months maximum.

Cookies and audience measurement

On your first visit, a consent banner lets you accept or refuse non-essential trackers (audience measurement). No consent-based tracker is placed until you agree. Cookies strictly necessary for the site are exempt. You can change or withdraw your choice at any time via the banner.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, objection, restriction and portability. To exercise them, write to barpixrepair@gmail.com.
