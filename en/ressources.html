What is an internal capture card?

An internal capture card is a component built inside your console. Unlike external boxes, it needs no extra accessory and offers identical quality for a much cleaner setup.

📺

Streaming from a modded console

With an integrated capture card, you simply plug a USB cable from your console into your PC, open OBS and you're ready to stream. No latency on the console's screen.
