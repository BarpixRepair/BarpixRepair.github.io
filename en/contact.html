// FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I send my console?
Once the quote is approved, you send your console by tracked post to the address I give you. Pack it carefully with bubble wrap. I let you know as soon as it arrives and keep you updated throughout the work.
How long does a capture card install take?
Usually between 48h and 5 working days depending on the model and the workload. The exact lead time is given with the quote.
Does the capture card install void the warranty?
Yes, opening the console voids the manufacturer's warranty. That's inherent to this kind of hardware mod. However, I guarantee my work: if an issue is linked to my intervention, I take care of it.
Which software works with the capture card?
The installed capture card works with OBS Studio (free), Elgato 4K Capture Utility, AverMedia RECentral and most capture software on PC and Mac. I provide a getting-started guide with every install.
Do you repair very old consoles?
Depending on the fault, yes! Contact me with a description of the problem. I'll tell you honestly whether it's within my reach and whether the repair is worth it.
How does payment work?
Payment is made after the work, when your console is returned. I don't require a deposit for small repairs. For bigger projects a 30% deposit may be requested. Payment by bank transfer or PayPal.
