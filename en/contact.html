// FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I send my console?
You can ship it as follows:
• By post directly to my address shown on the quote.
• or via Mondial Relay (LIDL Locker or Games Factory Locker in Beaune)
• or via Chronopost (Chez Coco in Bligny-les-Beaune)

Please pack it well with a sturdy box, bubble wrap and quality tape. Feel free to photograph your parcel before sending, and include my email and phone for tracking.
Which software works with the capture card?
The software to use is 3DS Capture or cc3dsfs, preferred when using several consoles. Download them here: 3DS Capture and cc3dsfs.
How does payment work?
Payment is always made by bank transfer before work begins, as stated on the quotes. If your project ends up costing less, I refund the difference or deduct it from your next order. The bank details are at the bottom of every quote.
